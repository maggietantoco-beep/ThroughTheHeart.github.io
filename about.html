~ About our website ~

• What is Through the Heart? •


- Inspired by themes of The Little Prince, Through The Heart is an informative website that features important topics and resources about problems found in all kinds of communities. Whether it be bullying, suicide, or others, this website collects valid information and compiles it into one cohesive and easy-to-navigate website for people of all kinds to access.

• Why was Through The Heart made? •


- Through The Heart was created to be a safe space where people can learn about various social challenges like bullying, suicide, and others. It was made to break the silence, raise awareness, and offer a hand to those who struggle with these challenges, while encouraging people to exercise their empathy and emotions. Not only that, but this website was also formed to remind people that true understanding starts within the heart.

Whether you're here to learn, share, or lend a hand, know this:
change starts not just with the mind, but
through the heart.
